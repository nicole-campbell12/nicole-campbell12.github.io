Hidden Tank Fire Table Roundup

Hey friends, this post may contain affiliate links. As an Amazon Affiliate, I earn a very small percentage from qualifying purchases made via links from this site, at no cost to you. I only recommend products that I love and trust.

I’ve been searching for the perfect fire table for our backyard, especially right now when the nights are chilly and we still want to spend time outside on our patio.

Most of the fire tables I see have a separate ‘cover’ for the exterior propane tank and a visible hose connecting to the fire table, which doesn’t really work well for the space where I’m going to set it up.

All of the fire table choices I’ve rounded up so far have a sleek and modern design that hides the propane tank inside! Most also have a cover for the center so that it can act as a full table top when not in use, which I really love.

I think I’ve collected some great choices - my top picks are 1, 2, 3 and 10. Now I just need to choose one!
